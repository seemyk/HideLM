# Privacy Policy

No Featured NotebookLM does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

## Data collection

- No analytics
- No tracking
- No cookies
- No external network requests

## Permissions

The extension runs only on `https://notebooklm.google.com/*` and injects CSS to hide
specific UI elements.
