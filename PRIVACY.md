# Privacy Policy

HideLM does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. This policy applies to the Chrome/Chromium and Firefox versions of the extension.

## Data collection

- No analytics
- No tracking
- No cookies
- No external network requests

## Permissions

The extension runs only on `https://notebooklm.google.com/*` and injects CSS to hide
specific UI elements.
